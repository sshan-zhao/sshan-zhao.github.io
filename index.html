Research

I'm interested in: computer vision (2D image understanding), deep learning (domain adaptation/generalization), 3D vision (point cloud, depth), and AIGC (text-to-image, diffusion model). Currently, I mainly focus on 3D vision perception/understanding and controllable text-to-image generation/editing.
