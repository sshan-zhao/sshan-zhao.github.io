Bio

Greetings! I am Shanshan Zhao (Chinese: 赵杉杉). Currently, I am a PhD Candidate at The University of Sydney (USYD, from July 2018), under the supervision of Prof. Dacheng Tao. I received my master's degree from College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University in 2017 (supervised by Prof. Xi Li), and bachelor's degree in Network Engineering (Outstanding Engineer Plan) from Xidian University in 2014. My works focus on optical flow estimation, depth estimation, and 3D vision.
